# 🩸 Blood Donation Management System

## 📌 Purpose of the Project

The purpose of this project is to develop a digital system that helps manage blood donation activities between donors, blood banks, and hospitals. Blood shortages are a serious challenge in many healthcare systems, and manual processes often lead to delays and poor coordination.

This project aims to provide a centralized platform where blood banks can efficiently manage donors, blood collection, laboratory testing, and hospital blood requests. The system helps improve coordination and ensures that safe blood is available when it is needed.

---

## ⚙️ What the System Will Do

The Blood Donation Management System will support the complete workflow of blood donation and distribution.

Key functionalities include:

- **User Management**  
  The system supports multiple roles including donors, blood bank administrators, hospital administrators, blood collectors, and lab technicians.

- **Blood Donation Management**  
  Manage the process of blood donation from donor screening to blood collection and testing.

- **Blood Inventory Management**  
  Track available blood units, their types, collection dates, and expiration dates.

- **Hospital Blood Requests**  
  Allow hospitals to request blood units and track the status of their requests.

- **Emergency Blood Alerts**  
  Notify eligible donors when there is an urgent need for a specific blood type.

- **Donation Campaign Management**  
  Allow blood banks to create and manage blood donation campaigns.

Overall, the system improves communication and coordination between donors, blood banks, and hospitals.
